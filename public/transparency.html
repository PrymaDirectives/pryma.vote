Transparency & Threat Notes

This poll is constitutional, not a marketing funnel. Below is an exact list of what is collected and how it is stored.

Data flow

  • Pure static HTML/CSS/JS: no npm, no server code, no third-party embeds or analytics.
  • Your responses are stored locally in your browser under localStorage; you choose when to export them.
  • Optional proof-of-work still runs locally; the receipt is saved with your submission for transparency.
  • Optional pseudonym key and signed message are stored alongside metadata, not mixed with answers.
  • Optional client-side encryption stores ciphertext + IV + salt; answers remain unreadable without your passphrase.

Stored format

  "storedAt": "2024-01-01T00:00:00.000Z",
  "metadata": {

  },
  "answers": { "trustPrimitive": "A living human", ... },
  "encryptedPayload": null
}

Threat considerations

  • Linkability: No long-term identifiers; optional encryption prevents even server-side reading.
  • Coercion: No binding to accounts; responses can be made offline and uploaded via Tor or similar.
  • Manipulation: Optional PoW and dedupe hash help you police spam before exporting data.
  • Transparency: The full collection policy is shown on this page and inside the static bundle.
  • Accessibility: All controls are keyboard-navigable; mobile-friendly layout.
